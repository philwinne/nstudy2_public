To create a Note in nStudy:

  1. Select text you want to make notes on or comment on

  2. A menu box will appear, click on "Create Note"



  3. A text box will open, click in the text space


  4. A menu box with different types of note templates will appear

  5. Click on the type of note you want to create





  6. A template will open. Title your note, then fill in the rest of the text boxes


  7. You could also add a tag to your note as “ important ” or “to be reviewed ” . . . etc .
