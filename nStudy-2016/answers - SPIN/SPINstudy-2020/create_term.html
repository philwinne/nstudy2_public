You can create a term by following these steps:
  1. Mod-click anywhere on your map



  2. Choose “Term” from the “Create Item” context menu


  3. Type over “untitled term”to write your term



  4. Type in the definition of your term beside “Definition. . . ”



    5. Tip: If you need more space, you can double click on the book icon next to the title of your term. This will open the term in a new window. .
