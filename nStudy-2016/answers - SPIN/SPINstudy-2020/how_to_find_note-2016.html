In the “Studying view,” to return to a note:

  1. A nub for your note appears in the gutter on the right side of your webpage



    2. Clicking on the nub shows you the note you have made, as well as the highlighted text on the page.



  2. The note also appears in the “Side bar ” on the left side of the webpage

    3. To access the Side bar, click on the double arrows on the left side of your webpage.
