Tags are indexes. You can generate a system for cataloging quotes according to tasks (seminar, lab6), qualities of information (vague, metaphor), follow-ups (explore, ask prof.), and so forth.

  1. Once you choose “Create Term...”, you will see the Term template on the right side of the screen. You will have the option to tag your Term.

  2. You can tag the quote associated with your term, or tag the Term itself.


  3. You can choose a tag from the pre-set menu, or type in your own tag.

    Click in the tag space to see the pre-set tags available.




  4. The tags you have entered will now appear with your Term.
