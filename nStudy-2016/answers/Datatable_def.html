To add items to a Folder:

  1. Click nStudy icon then choose" Open Library"



  2. The library view will open. Click the arrow next to "My Library"


  3. A list of all items you created will appear. The most recent created Folder will appear at the bottom of the list


  4. Locate the item you want to add to Folder

  5. Click and hold down the mouse to grab the item



  6. Drag the item to your Folder

  7. Drop the item by releasing the mouse. The item will appear under the destination Folder
