The sidebar is a panel on the left side of your window you can open and close. The sidebar is divided into sections by headers. Headers name artifacts in this view. On the right edge of the sidebar, icons for different views are listed. To open a view, click on its icon. .

How to search the Sidebar?

Search for Content:

  1. Type words you want to find. Numbers on the right side of headers show how many of those items contain what you typed.
  2. To see specific artifacts containing what you searched for,click the triangles in the header.


  3. Clicking on a particular item in the sidebar opens it.

  1. Type in the tag(s) you are looking for.
