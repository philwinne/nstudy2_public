To create a note in nStudy:

  1. You can start to make a note by selecting text or just right clicking

  2. A popup menu will appear. Click on "Create Note"


  3. A popup menu will appear with kinds of notes you can choose. Click on the kind of note you want to create

  4. A note window will open. Label your note to give it a title, then fill in the rest of the information in text boxes

  5. If you need help filling out a note or a part of it, click the help button


  6. You can add a tag to your note, for example, "important" or “to be reviewed”
