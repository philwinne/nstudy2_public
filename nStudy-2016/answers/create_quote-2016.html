If you can select text in the browser, this text can be saved as a quote. To add a quote :
  1. Go to any webpage
  2. Click at the beginning of text you want to quote then drag through it to select it
  3. Once you select it, you will see a context menu. Choose “ Create Quote... ”


  4. A nub for this quote will appear in the gutter on the right end of your webpage

    5. Tip: When you hover over a nub in the gutter, a banner unfolds showing the first few words of the quote you created .



  5. The quote will also appear in the “Map View ” on the map of the bookmark saved




  6. To open your quote in the “ Map view ”, drag and drop your quote in the map, then click it.
