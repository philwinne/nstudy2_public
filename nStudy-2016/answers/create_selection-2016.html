If you can select text in the browser, this text can be saved as a quote. To add a quote :
  1. Go to any webpage
  2. Click at the beginning of text you want to quote then drag through it to select it
  3. Once you select it, you will see a context menu. Choose “Create Quote...”




  4. Once you choose “Create Quote...”, you will have the option to tag your quote. You can choose a tag from the pre-set menu, or type in your own tag.



    Click in the tag space to see the pre-set tags available.


  5. When you are done tagging, click “Create” to see your quote.

  8. The quote will also appear in the “Side bar ” on the left side of the webpage
