You can compute descriptive statistics by following these steps:
  1. Click on the analysis button to open a menu with options.

  2. Choose "Descriptive"

  3. You can type in the Number of Bins field to control the number of bins for the histogram.


  4. Choose the variable to analyze, then click Submit.

  5. A window appears with a Histogram, Summary statistics and Quantiles (Descriptive Stat.)
