1. Click on the analysis button to open a drop down menu with options.
  2. Choose Fit Y by X. NOTE: Scales (continuous/categorical) of X and Y variables determine analysis type.
  3. Click on a variable in the list on the left to identify the Y (Outcome) variable, then click the button Y, Outcome.
  4. Click a variable in the list at left to identify the X (Factor) variable, then click the button X, Factor.
  5. Click Submit.
In the Fit Y by X analysis view, you see the plot of data and Summary Statistics.

What are the graph options?
  1. Hover over or click elements in the graphical display to reveal more information.
  2. Click Select Display Options to view additions to the graphical display.
  3. Click on histogram bars to select cells in the data table. Use right-click (Chromebook win) or cmd-click to select multiple bars.
  4. Display confidence regions with checkboxes (Linear, quadratic, cubic only).
  5. Set alpha (confidence) level with dropdown next to checkboxes.
  6. Outputs for fits can be collapsed and expanded by clicking on aret icons (>).
  7. Hover over {...} next to Save Values to Table to reveal a dropdown that allows you to save.
