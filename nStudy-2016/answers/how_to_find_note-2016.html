There are several ways to open a note:

  1. A nub for each note appears in the gutter on the right side of your window.


  2. Clicking the nub opens and shows the associated note.

  3. The label you used to title the note also appears in the sidebar under the header “Notes”.
  4. To access the sidebar, click on the handle.


  5. Click the Notes header to show notes in this view.


  6. Clicking a particular note in the side bar will open the note and show its selection if there is one.
