There are several ways how to open a note

  1. A nub for each note appears in the gutter on the right side of your window



  2. Clicking the nub opens and shows its associated note


The label you used to title the note also appears in the “Side bar ” under the header “Notes ”


  1. To access the Side bar, click on the handle



  2. Click on the triangle in the Notes header to show notes in this view


  3. Clicking a particular note in the side bar will open the note and show its selection if there is one ADD SCREENSHOT #8
