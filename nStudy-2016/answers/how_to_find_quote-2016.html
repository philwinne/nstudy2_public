To open a selection in the “Study View” :

  1. A nub for your selection appears in the gutter on the right side of your webpage



    2. Clicking on the nub shows you the selection you have made, as well as the highlighted text on the page.



  2. The selection also appears in the “Side bar ” on the left side of the webpage

    3. To access the Sidebar, click on the handle on the left side of your webpage.
