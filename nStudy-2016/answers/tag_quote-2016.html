A tag is a word or phrase you use to classify information, e.g. interesting, hypothesis; plan for a task, e.g., review, look up,...etc. nStudy assigns an “Important” tag for you. Change it to use a more helpful tag.

  1. Once you choose “Create Selection...”, you will have the option to tag your selection.
  2. Click in the tag box to see tags already created. You can create a new tag by typing in the tag box, then hitting Enter.
