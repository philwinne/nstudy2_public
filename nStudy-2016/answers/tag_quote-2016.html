A tag is A word or phrase you use to classify information, e.g. interesting, hypothesis; plan for a task, e.g., review, look up,...etc. SPInStudy assigns an “Important” tag for you. Change it to use a more helpful tag.

  1. Once you choose “Create Selection...”, you will have the option to tag your selection.

      Why tag selections ? Tagging helps build understanding, makes this information easy to find later using sidebar search or faceted search].
