A tag is a word or phrase you use to classify information, e.g., interesting, hypothesis; plan for a task, e.g., review, look up, etc. SPInStudy assigns an “Important” tag for you. Change the tag to use a more helpful tag.

  1. Once you choose "Create Term..." you will see the term form on the right side of the screen. You will have the option to tag your term.
  2. When you open a term you can enter tags in the text box titled "Tags".
  3. You can choose a tag from the popup menu, or type in your own tag. Click in the tag space to see the tags available.
